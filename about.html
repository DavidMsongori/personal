MY STORY

A Journey of Leadership, Service and Impact

My journey has always been driven by a passion for empowering young people, strengthening communities, and creating opportunities that inspire sustainable development.

As an Agricultural Scientist, Youth Leader, Climate Advocate and Public Speaker, I have dedicated my career to promoting transformational leadership, climate resilience, education, innovation and strategic partnerships that create lasting impact across Kenya.

Through leadership in regional and national organizations, I continue to champion youth participation, policy engagement, environmental conservation and inclusive economic empowerment.
